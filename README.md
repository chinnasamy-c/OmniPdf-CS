# OmniPdf CS 📄✨

OmniPdf CS is a comprehensive, all-in-one PDF utility and productivity toolkit designed to give you complete control over your documents. Packed with 21 powerful tools organized into three easy-to-use sections, it eliminates the need for multiple apps.

## 🚀 Key Features & Tools

### 📁 Section: Managing PDF Pages, PDF Security & Identity, Creation & Editing
Effortlessly organize and structure your document layouts:
1. **Merge PDF** – Combine multiple files into a single document.
2. **Split PDF** – Divide large files into smaller, separate PDFs.
3. **Delete and Reorder Pages** – Remove unwanted pages or rearrange their order.
4. **Rotate PDF** – Fix orientation issues instantly.
5. **Resize PDF** – Compress or change the layout size of your pages.
6. **Pages per Sheet** – Format multiple pages to fit on a single sheet.
7. **Watermark PDF** – Protect your intellectual property with custom watermarks.
8. **Lock PDF** – Add strong password protection to sensitive files.
9. **Unlock PDF** – Access password-protected files quickly.
10. **Remove Restrictions** – Strip away editing, printing, or copying limitations.
11. **Metadata Remover** – Clean hidden tracking data and history from your files.
12. **Color Inverted** – Switch to high-contrast or dark modes for easy viewing.
13. **Signature Maker** – Create and apply your digital signature seamlessly.
14. **Filter PDF** – Apply visual filters to enhance document readability.
15. **Add Page Numbering** – Keep your documents organized with structured headers/footers.
16. **Repair PDF** – Fix broken or corrupted PDF files instantly.
17. **Resume Maker** – Build professional CVs and resumes directly within the app.
18. **Auto Scroll PDF** – Enjoy hands-free reading with smooth automated scrolling.
19. **Draw on PDF** – Annotate, sketch, or write freely on any document page.
20. **Auto Tag PDF** – Structure your document tags automatically for better accessibility.
21. **Stamp Badge** – Place badges or standard business stamps on your files.
